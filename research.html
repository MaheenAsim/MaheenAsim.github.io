Future Research Focus

Key research themes and questions that will guide my doctoral studies and future academic career

Machine Learning & Optimization

  • • How can we develop self-supervised or semi-supervised deep learning models for fault prediction in manufacturing systems that continuously adapt to drifts in data distribution without requiring extensive new labeled data?
  • • What are the theoretical foundations for adaptive learning in dynamic environments?
  • • How can Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning (MARL) be used to design decentralized control policies for complex, heterogeneous production lines, ensuring system-level stability and throughput maximization despite machine failures and stochastic processing times?

Industrial Systems Engineering

  • • How can we use Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) and dynamic network analysis to model multi-echelon supply chains, predict cascading failure risks from disruptive events, and prescribe robust re-routing and re-stocking strategies?
  • • What role can AI play in sustainable manufacturing and energy systems?How can computer vision and natural language processing be used to automatically classify, grade, and disassemble returned electronic products to maximize value recovery and minimize waste in reverse supply chains?
  • • Can we develop causal machine learning models that disentangle the effects of promotions, competitor actions, and macroeconomic shocks on demand forecasts to improve inventory optimization and reduce bullwhip effects?
  • • How can we build a "digital twin" of a hospital emergency department using a hybrid approach that combines discrete-event simulation with real-time, data-driven predictive models (for patient inflow, acuity, and length-of-stay) to proactively test and optimize staffing and resource allocation policies for both efficiency and equitable care delivery under stress scenarios?

Education & Technology

  • • What are the formal frameworks for integrating imperfect human feedback (e.g., from a factory manager or a logistics planner) into sequential decision-making AI systems to correct for model bias and adapt to unmodeled objectives?
  • • Can we create a multimodal reinforcement learning agent that personalizes learning pathways by synthesizing data from a student's clickstream, written responses, and (with appropriate privacy safeguards) speech or facial cues to infer engagement and confusion in real-time?
  • • Using techniques from survival analysis and graph-based feature extraction from campus data, can we build an early-warning system for student dropout that is not only predictive but also explainable to advisors, highlighting the key contributing factors for each student?

Data Science & Analytics

  • • How can we create optimal resource allocation and scheduling systems for large-scale inference servers that dynamically batch requests and scale resources to minimize latency and cost while serving thousands of concurrent machine learning models?
  • • How can we use Bayesian optimization and optimal experimental design to proactively guide data collection efforts, identifying the minimal set of most informative data points to acquire for model improvement, thereby reducing labeling and collection costs
  • • How can we develop robust causal machine learning models that estimate the heterogeneous treatment effects of complex interventions (e.g., a new drug or therapy) from noisy, high-dimensional Electronic Health Record (EHR) data, while accounting for unmeasured confounding and informative censoring?

## Page Content Structure

### index.html - Academic Portfolio Landing
**Purpose**: Create strong first impression for recruiters and admissions committees
**Sections**:
- Navigation bar with smooth scrolling
- Hero section with generated research visualization background
- Animated introduction with name and academic focus
- Research highlights carousel (8 major projects)
- Skills proficiency matrix visualization
- Academic achievements timeline
- Call-to-action for research collaboration

### research.html - Research Projects Deep Dive
**Purpose**: Showcase technical expertise and research methodology
**Sections**:
- Research project gallery with filter system
- Detailed project cards with methodology, results, GitHub links
- Publication showcase with conference presentations
- Technical skills visualization
- Research impact metrics
- Collaboration opportunities

### about.html - Academic Journey
**Purpose**: Present comprehensive academic background and leadership
**Sections**:
- Educational timeline with key milestones
- Leadership positions and awards
- Conference presentations and achievements
- Academic service and community involvement
- Personal research statement
- Future academic goals

### contact.html - Professional Networking
**Purpose**: Facilitate academic and professional connections
**Sections**:
- Contact information and academic profiles
- Research collaboration interests
- Office hours booking system
- Academic social media links
- Location and availability
- Research supervision interests

## Interactive Components

### 1. Research Project Filter System
- Filter by research area (ML, NLP, Computer Vision, Data Science)
- Sort by date, impact, or technical complexity
- Search functionality for specific topics

### 2. Skills Proficiency Radar Chart
- Interactive visualization of technical competencies
- Category filtering (Programming, ML, Data Science, Tools)
- Hover details with experience level and projects

### 3. Academic Timeline
- Interactive timeline with clickable milestones
- Achievement markers with detailed information
- Smooth animations and transitions

### 4. Publication Gallery
- Interactive cards with abstracts and conference details
- Presentation viewer for conference slides
- Download links for papers and supplementary materials

## Technical Implementation

### Core Libraries
- **Anime.js**: Smooth transitions and micro-interactions
- **ECharts.js**: Research data visualization and academic charts
- **p5.js**: Creative coding background effects
- **Splitting.js**: Typography effects for headings
- **Splide.js**: Image carousels for research projects

### Visual Effects
- Particle system background representing neural networks
- Staggered text animations for academic achievements
- Interactive data visualizations
- Smooth scroll motion with parallax effects
- 3D hover effects on project cards

### Responsive Design
- Mobile-first approach for academic networks
- Optimized loading for research institutions
- High-contrast accessibility for academic environments
- Print-friendly styling for CV generation

This structure creates a comprehensive academic portfolio that effectively showcases research capabilities while maintaining professional credibility for graduate school applications and research-focused career opportunities.
